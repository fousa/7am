7am

A Nespresso© blend chooser for iPhone.

Would you like not to have to choose which Nespresso© ristretto, espresso, lungo or cappuccino you have to drink today? Well, there is an app for that!

Meet 7am, our first tiny little iPhone app. What does it do? Not much really. Once started - add some iPhone magic here - it shows you a random Nespresso© blend with its strongness and size. Not in the mood for this one? Shake (if activated) or tap the screen and it will flip to another one. You can select which blends you have around to limit your selection.
